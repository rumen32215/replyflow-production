# ReplyFlow Dashboard V2

## Vision

ReplyFlow should feel like a business assistant, not dashboard software.

When a tradesperson opens the app they should immediately know:

"What needs my attention first?"

The dashboard should never overwhelm the user with charts, numbers or reports.

Instead it should guide them through their day.

---

# Core Principles

1. Action before analytics.

2. Outcomes before technology.

3. Calm, premium and simple.

4. Every card should help the business owner earn more money, save time or avoid missing work.

---

# ReplyFlow Voice

Never sell AI.

Sell outcomes.

Avoid:

❌ AI generated quote

Use:

✅ Quote ready to send

Avoid:

❌ AI answered 14 enquiries

Use:

✅ ReplyFlow looked after 14 enquiries

Avoid:

❌ AI summary

Use:

✅ Morning summary

Avoid:

❌ AI Receptionist replied

Use:

✅ ReplyFlow replied

Avoid:

❌ AI saved 2 hours

Use:

✅ You got 2 hours back

---

# Dashboard Priority

1. Morning Summary

2. Urgent actions

3. Quick actions

4. Today's activity

5. Recent conversations

6. Customers

7. Wins today

# Dashboard Goal

Every section should answer:

"What should I do next?"

Not:

"What happened?"
